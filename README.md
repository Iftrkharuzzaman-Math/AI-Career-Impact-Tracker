# AI-Career-Impact-Tracker
An interactive web tool that helps professionals understand how Artificial Intelligence might impact their roles based on current research and industry data.

# Purpose
The AI Career Impact Tracker is designed to:
1. Educate professionals about AI's potential impact on various occupations
2. Provide data-driven insights based on recent research from leading institutions
3. Help individuals make informed decisions about career development and skill acquisition
4. Visualize AI exposure across different sectors and roles
5. Promote awareness of how AI is transforming the workplace

# AI Risk Index (ARI) Framework
The calculator uses a task-based exposure model that evaluates roles based on:
1. Task Automatability - Percentage of routine tasks that can be automated
2. AI Capability Overlap - Current AI capabilities vs. role requirements
3. Skill Complexity - Level of human judgment, creativity, and emotional intelligence required
4. Physical Component - Extent of physical/manual work involved
5. Decision-Making Level - Strategic vs. operational decision requirements

# Scoring System
1. 0-35%: Low Impact - Roles requiring high human judgment, physical presence, or emotional intelligence
2. 36-70%: Moderate Impact - Hybrid roles where AI augments rather than replaces
3. 71-100%: High Impact - Roles with significant automatable tasks

#Confidence Intervals
1. Established Roles: 85-95% confidence (tech, finance, admin)
2. Emerging Roles: 75-85% confidence (AI-adjacent positions)
3. Margin of Error: ±5-8% at 95% confidence interval

#Features
1. Sector-Based Navigation: Choose from 7 major industry sectors
2. Role-Specific Analysis: Detailed impact scores for 17 representative roles
3. Visual Indicators: Color-coded risk badges (High/Moderate/Low)
4. Contextual Descriptions: Explanatory text for each role's AI exposure
5. Research Citations: Transparent data sourcing and methodology
